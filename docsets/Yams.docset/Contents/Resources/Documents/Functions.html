    where Objects: Sequence

    Parameters

    Sequence of Objects.

    The intendation increment.

    Preferred line break.
    explicitStart

    Explicit document start ---.
    explicitEnd

    version

    YAML version directive.
    sortKeys

    Whether or not to sort Mapping keys in lexicographic order.
